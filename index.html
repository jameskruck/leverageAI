GenAI in Teaching and Learning

Are you curious about how your students might be using GenAI? Are you wondering how you might integrate GenAI into your classroom? Are you thinking about how you might assess your students knowing that they're using GenAI?

This workshop will introduce you to how GenAI might be used in your classrooms. You'll get a chance to experiment with GenAI and explore its capabilities. At the end of the session, you will walk away with a plan to implement GenAI in your classroom.

Our first step is to see how we can use GenAI as a collaborator. In this activity, we're going to see if GenAI can come up with a creative introduction for ourselves.

Example: The Iterative Process

See how an introduction evolves through multiple interactions:

Initial Prompt:

"Write a movie trailer intro about someone who loves gardening and just learned to make pottery."

First Response:

🎬 In a world of concrete and glass, one person dared to get their hands dirty. From nurturing seeds to shaping clay, they're about to discover that growth takes many forms...

Refinement Prompt:

"That's good! Could you make it more specific about how gardening and pottery connect?"

Improved Response:

🎬 In a world of concrete and glass, one person discovered the ancient magic of earth itself. By day, they coax life from soil in their flourishing garden. By night, they transform raw clay into vessels worthy of their homegrown harvests. Two crafts, one element, endless possibilities...

Final Refinement:

"Love it! Could you add something about sharing these creations with others?"

Final Version:

🎬 In a world of concrete and glass, one person discovered the ancient magic of earth itself. By day, they coax life from soil in their flourishing garden. By night, they transform raw clay into vessels worthy of their homegrown harvests. Now, they're bringing people together, one handcrafted pot of homegrown herbs at a time. Two crafts, one element, and a community growing stronger through the power of creation...

Helpful Refinement Prompts

For Adding Detail

  • • "Could you add more specific details about...?"
  • • "How could we incorporate [specific element]?"
  • • "Can you expand on the part about...?"

For Changing Style

  • • "Could you make it more [dramatic/funny/serious]?"
  • • "Try using the style of [genre/author/show]"
  • • "Can we adjust the tone to be more...?"
