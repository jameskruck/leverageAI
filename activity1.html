Document Your Process

Part One: Initial Proposal

To begin your Persuasion Impossible journey, you'll need to identify a persuasive challenge that stretches your abilities and plan how you and AI will collaborate to tackle it.

  • Select a persuasive task that genuinely feels difficult or impossible for you
  • Define clear roles and expectations for the human-AI collaboration
  • Establish specific success metrics to evaluate your outcomes

The activities below will help you develop your initial proposal (200-400 words) that outlines your challenge, collaboration approach, and success metrics.

Step 1: Identify Your Persuasive Challenge

Choose a persuasive task that will stretch or exceed your current persuasive abilities. Consider challenges such as:

  • Convincing a skeptical audience about a complex or controversial topic
  • Creating persuasive content for an unfamiliar medium or format
  • Crafting arguments for a position you personally find difficult to support
  • Developing persuasive messages for audiences with vastly different values or perspectives

Here's how a human-AI collaboration might work for a persuasive challenge:

I need to create a compelling environmental proposal for city council members who are skeptical about climate initiatives. I struggle with translating technical information into persuasive policy language.
I can help with that challenge. Let's break down our approach: 1. First, what environmental proposal are you supporting specifically? 2. What do we know about the council members' priorities (economic, safety, etc.)? 3. What technical information will we need to translate? I can help by: - Reframing technical concepts in accessible terms - Suggesting appeals based on different value frameworks - Helping structure arguments in a persuasive sequence
I want to propose a green infrastructure initiative. The council members care most about economic development and budget constraints. I have scientific data on stormwater management, urban heat reduction, and biodiversity, but I'm not sure how to make it compelling.
Perfect! Let's reframe your green infrastructure initiative through an economic lens: Key message framework: 1. Cost savings: How green infrastructure reduces long-term infrastructure costs 2. Economic development: How these projects create local jobs and business opportunities 3. Property value increases: Data on how urban green spaces improve property values 4. Disaster mitigation: Financial benefits of reduced flood damage For your stormwater data, we could translate this into: "Every $1 invested in green stormwater infrastructure saves $4 in prevented flood damage and reduced gray infrastructure maintenance." Would you like me to help craft specific economic arguments using your technical data?

Step 4: Document Your AI Interactions

For your project, you'll need to log at least five of your interactions with AI using this format:

  • Date: When the interaction occurred
  • AI Tool Used: Which AI system you worked with
  • Purpose: Goal of the interaction (e.g., "Brainstorm persuasive angles" or "Get feedback on draft")
  • Highlights: Key prompts and responses from the interaction
  • Key Takeaways: What you learned about AI collaboration
