Authentic Assessment Strategies

Rather than trying to "AI-proof" traditional assignments, consider authentic assessments that naturally encourage original thinking and real-world application:

Case Studies

Students analyze real scenarios and propose solutions

Example: Marketing students create campaign for local non-profit

Portfolio Development

Document process and progress over time

Example: Engineering students showing design iterations

Community Projects

Engage with actual community needs

Example: Urban planning solutions for local issues

Research Projects

Original research with real data

Example: Analyzing local environmental data

Key Benefits

  • ✓ Naturally resistant to AI-generated content
  • ✓ Develops real-world skills
  • ✓ Encourages critical thinking
  • ✓ Promotes student engagement
  • ✓ Supports diverse learning styles
  • ✓ More equitable assessment

Assignment Design

Design assignments that:

  • Connect to real-world contexts
  • Encourage personal reflection
  • Value process as well as product
  • Build transferable skills
  • Allow creative approaches

Student Guidance
